About BeLively

Professional Studio Classes

Our online studio classes are taught by certified trained professionals. Be Lively has an instructor for any type of workout exercise.

One on One Sessions

Set a time to have a one on one with an assigned personal trainer. Our instructors actively monitor their students to make sure they are staying active and healthy.

Daily Videos for Everyone

BeLively has new videos every day for every time of workout exercise. Our app allows you to search for your instructor or filter for your favorite category.

Track Your Progress

Keep track of your daily calories and weight through our app. Show off your results to your instructor and prove the exercises are working.

Equipment Rentals

Rent your workout equipment through us and we will make sure you are always prepared for any type of workout class.

BeLively Team
